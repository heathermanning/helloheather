Faba


Context
Faba is an app that facilitates financial literacy in teens and tweens while reducing the hassle of managing allowance for parents.

Problem
Kids and teens have little to no resources available to begin fostering healthy money management skills early in life. Many kids and teens earn money through odd jobs, allowances, or gifts and have no tools in place to set savings goals or simply track their money. Furthermore, having cash on hand for allowances is inconvenient for parents as most transactions are made via card.

Process
We conducted two weeks of user research, which involved interviews with parents and teens to better understand behaviors around allowance, money management, and spending, as well as research into behavioural predictions of Generation Z and Generation Alpha—our target demographic. We then began prototyping an app that would meet the needs of our primary user, the young earner, and be a useful tool for parents and guardians.

Solution
The end product includes separate parent and teen profiles, transferring and budgeting functionality, plus a visual design and content strategy that matures with the user from the preteen years through late adolescence.
