Polo and Pan Album Cover


Context
This album design project was assigned in a microtypography class. The brief was to choose an album and redesign the cover.

Concept
Polo and Pan are a french electronic duo who aim to express through their music what Henri Rousseau shared in his paintings: “The quest for a wild life, a return to the primitive forms of happiness, and a colorful ode to idleness.”

I wanted to capture this essence in my redesign of their album Canopee, while also embodying the sense of mounting tension and expansive release present in their music.

A fruitful brainstorming session provided many possible visual directions, one being the idea of climbing a mountain and the expansive views one receives once they reach the summit. I wanted to include both natural and constructed elements as a nod towards the fusion of electronic music alongside bird calls and sounds of the jungle that can be found throughout Polo and Pan’s work.

The final product embodies the experience of climbing towards and building up to an eruption of nature; a passageway to abundance.


disciplines: art direction, visual design
