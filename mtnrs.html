The Mountaineers


I worked with a team of consultants on the facilitation of a strategic planning process for a local non-profit, The Mountaineers. The culminating document of the year-long process was the Progressive Climbing Education report, a fifty-page document describing the next chapter of the 100-year old nonprofit. I developed materials that were used throughout the process including iconagraphy to represent the core offerings of The Mountaineers, one-pagers, and presentations.
